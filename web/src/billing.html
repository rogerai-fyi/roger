      Where your money goes

      Every request is metered, priced, and debited from your wallet - and every change is written to an append-only ledger you can audit.

      1. Metered Each request counts the tokens going in and coming out.
      2. Priced Those tokens are priced at the model's posted rate ($ per 1M in + out).
      3. Debited That cost is debited from your wallet balance.
      4. Ledgered Every top-up and charge is written to an append-only ledger - entries are never edited or deleted.
      5. Verified "Verified" re-sums that whole ledger to re-derive your balance, as a drift check. It should always match Balance.
      6. Paid out If you run a node, your operator earnings accrue on the same ledger and pay out to you.
