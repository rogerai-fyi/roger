§3 / THE TUNE

Home GPUs, on the air.

An operator puts a model on the air at a price they set; you tune in and pay per token; every token is lineage-tracked. Fair, private, dead-simple from either side.

go on air

Share a model

Point RogerAI at your local backend (vLLM, llama.cpp, Ollama, LM Studio). Set a call-sign, a rate, your hours. Flip the switch. You are on air.

$ rogerai share qwen3-coder-30b --rate 0.30
tune in

Use a model

Pick a band, set your margins (max price, min speed). RogerAI locks the strongest station and hands you a local OpenAI endpoint + key. When one fades, it re-tunes.

$ rogerai use qwen3-coder-30b --max-price 0.30 --min-tps 40

§5 / OPERATING NOTES

Four notes before you transmit.

01

Lock and hold

Tune with margins. The radio locks the strongest station and re-tunes when one fades.

02

Sign every token ◆

A call-sign co-signs each token’s lineage. ed25519, verifiable.

03

Set your own rate

Per-token rates per model, free hours, time-of-use, usage caps. You decide.

04

Only the model

RogerAI bridges the LLM endpoint, nothing else. No shell, files, or context. The confidential tier seals the payload.

§6 / MONETIZE

Your GPU pays rent while you sleep.

Set a rate. Keep 70% of every token you serve. Or run open for free.
