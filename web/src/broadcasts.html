((•)) TELEGRAM FROM: THE FOUNDER · 2026

When I was growing up, I heard we would all have compute in our pockets. That came true. Now we have learned to turn that compute into intelligence, and that intelligence belongs to you, to everyone. The time has come to band together and create a new model in the open, because real intelligence is all of us building and sharing together.

The large labs are all monopolizing, and can rug-pull us at any moment. However, while the open airwaves stay open and free, we all can become our own frontier. Each signal, each band shared and borrowed, gives to the airwaves for an open market we all can benefit from.

I hope you enjoy this product, and can find more ways to connect ideas one band at a time. The band is now open - roger that.
