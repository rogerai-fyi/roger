Terms and Acceptable Use

The short version, written plainly. The lawyer-reviewed version supersedes this.

Money and payments

Stripe is the payments provider and the licensed money transmitter. RogerAI never takes custody of your funds outside Stripe. Operator payouts run through Stripe Connect after a hold period and a rolling reserve, and require Stripe KYC.

Acceptable use

No sanctioned jurisdictions or parties. No illegal content (including CSAM, which the broker screens for before dispatch). No prohibited content categories an operator has not opted into. We may pause payouts or accounts on a sanctions or fraud signal.

Disputes and chargebacks

The platform is liable for card chargebacks. Earnings are held and a reserve is kept back so a reversed consumer charge can be clawed back before money leaves Stripe.

Data and deletion

You can export your data and delete your account. Deletion anonymizes your identity but retains de-identified financial records for the legal retention window (tax/AML).
