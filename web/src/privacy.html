Privacy

What we collect, why, and how to get it out or wipe it. Written plainly.

Who we are

RogerAI is the platform behind rogerai.fyi and the broker at broker.rogerai.fyi. It routes prompts to self-hosted nodes and settles pay-per-token between consumers and node operators. This page covers the data the platform itself handles. Stripe (our payments provider) and GitHub (our login provider) handle data under their own policies, linked below.

Account and identity

You sign in with GitHub OAuth. From GitHub we receive and store your numeric GitHub user id and your login (handle), and - only if you choose to save one - a contact email. We do not receive or store your GitHub password. Your avatar is loaded directly from GitHub's public avatar CDN by your numeric id; we do not copy or host it.

Payments and payouts

Stripe is the payments provider and money transmitter. Top-ups run through Stripe Checkout and payouts through Stripe Connect. We never see, store, or process your card number, bank details, or KYC identity documents - Stripe holds all of that. On our side we keep only a wallet balance, a ledger of top-ups and spend, accrued earnings, and the Stripe account/reference ids needed to reconcile a payment.

Usage and lineage

When you send a request we record what is needed to bill it and to settle the operator: the model or node used, token counts, cost, and a timestamp. Settled requests produce a lineage receipt - a signed record of which node served which request - so both sides can audit the ledger. We do not sell your data or use prompt contents to train models.

Cookies

We use a single cookie: a signed, http-only session cookie set by the broker after you log in. It is not readable by JavaScript and no access tokens are ever exposed to the page. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies. Your browser may send GitHub's own cookies when loading your avatar from GitHub's CDN; that is GitHub's cookie, not ours.

What we log

Operationally we keep request and billing records (above), plus standard server logs needed to run and secure the service - for example error traces and abuse/fraud signals. We retain de-identified financial records for the legal retention window (tax and anti-money-laundering rules) even after an account is deleted.

Your data: export and delete

You control your data from the Account page.

Export. "Export my data" calls POST /account/export and downloads a JSON copy of the account data we hold for you.

Delete. "Delete account" calls POST /account/delete. It anonymizes your identity (your GitHub id, login, and email are removed or scrambled) while retaining de-identified financial records for the legal window described above. The action cannot be undone.

Third parties

The processors we rely on: GitHub (login and avatars), Stripe (payments, payouts, KYC), and our hosting provider (serving the site and broker). Each handles your data under its own privacy policy.

Contact

Questions, an export problem, or a data request: email privacy@rogerai.fyi. For security issues see the Security page.
