        §3 / THE TUNE
        
Home GPUs, on the air.

        
Powerful open models sit idle on home GPUs everywhere. RogerAI opens that silicon
          as one shared band: an operator puts a model on the air at a price they set, you
          tune in and pay per token, and every token is lineage-tracked. Fair, private, and
          dead-simple from either side.

      


      

        

          

            
            
            go on air
          

          
Share a model

          
Point RogerAI at your local backend (vLLM, llama.cpp, Ollama, LM Studio). Set a call-sign,
            a rate, and your hours. Flip the switch. You are on air.

          rogerai share waterfall --rate 0.30
        


        

          
        


        

          

            
            
            tune in
          

          
Use a model

          
Pick a band, set your margins (max price, min speed). RogerAI locks the strongest
            station and hands you a local OpenAI endpoint + key. When one fades, it re-tunes.

          rogerai use qwen3-coder-30b --max-price 0.30 --min-tps 40

        §5 / OPERATING NOTES
        
Four notes before you transmit.

      


      

        

          01
          
Lock and hold

          
Tune with margins. The radio locks the strongest station and re-tunes when one fades.

        

        

          02
          
Sign every token ◆

          
A gold call-sign co-signs each token's lineage. ed25519, verifiable.

        

        

          03
          
Set your own rate

          
Per-token rates per model, free hours, time-of-use, usage caps. You decide.

        

        

          04
          
Only the model

          
RogerAI bridges the LLM endpoint, nothing else. No shell, files, or context. The confidential tier seals the payload.
